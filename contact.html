Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available at checkout. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days depending on location.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! All orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping within the United States. International free shipping is available on orders over $150.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package in real time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption for your safety.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the contact form above, email us at support@glasscart.com, or call us at +1 (800) 123-4567 during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm PST).
